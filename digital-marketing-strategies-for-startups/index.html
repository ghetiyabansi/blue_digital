Popular Digital Marketing Strategies For Startups in India

Search Engine Optimization (SEO)

Search Engine Optimization refers to a set of practices that aim at increasing the visibility of a website on search engines like Google, Yahoo, and Bing. When a website ranks higher on a search engine, it gets more visibility which brings more traffic to the website, and ultimately more sales and engagement. 

SEO Strategy for Startups

To master this, search engine optimization experts search for the most effective keywords and key phrases that users type while doing the search. There are lots of SEO activities that are done both on the website (on-site) and other portals (off-site).

“The wording on your web pages, the way other sites link to you on the web, and the structure of your website are all factors in SEO.”

Since search engines like Google follow a certain algorithm, it is a challenge for SEO experts and digital marketers to optimize the website considering that algorithm. Some of the activities that are usually done to decode this algorithm are given below:

Content indexing – This is done to make it easier for search engines to catch the new content that is present on the website. The indexing includes adding alt text to images and adding text transcripts to audio and video.

Easy link structure — Search engines must be able to “crawl” your site structure when it comes to finding the content. An SEO strategist can style links, URLs, and sitemaps in a variety of ways to make them more accessible to site crawlers.

Targeted keywords – One of the essential building blocks of SEO is properly deploying your keywords – i.e., the search terms you want your site to be discovered for. The strategy of “stuffing” your content with as many keywords and keyword variations is no longer an effective way to get traction. Drafting value-based content with smart use of targeted keywords in the headers and a few times throughout the content is now considered a proven strategy. This way, your website pages will rank higher in search results.

How De SEO Services Work For Startups Work?

When it comes to giving the required exposure to your business or brand, SEO emerges as the prevalent and effective tool to count on. Also known as Search Engine Optimization, it involves a number of activities that bring organic traffic to your website without the help of paid marketing.

For example, if you launch a blog, search engines will direct organic traffic to your services pages, homepage, and landing pages. As a result, SEO enables small businesses to grow without spending any money on advertising.

  • High ROI and long-lasting results that are also cost-effective

Pay Per Click (PPC) Or Google Ads Marketing

Paid adverts and sponsored search engine results come under the purview of pay-per-click advertising. This type of advertising is based on a simple model, which means your ads won’t appear the moment you stop your payment. PPC, similar to SEO, is a proven strategy that is leveraged to improve a company’s web search traffic.

Google Ads Marketing Strategy For Startups

Advertisements that appear at the top and sides of a page of search results, ads that appear when browsing the web, commercials that appear before YouTube videos, and ads that appear in mobile apps are all examples of pay-per-click advertising.

Another feature that sets pay-per-click apart from SEO is that you just pay for the outcomes. In a conventional PPC arrangement, such as a Google AdWords campaign, you will only pay when someone clicks on your ad.

When a user clicks on your ad and visits your website PPC advertising allows you to spend virtually any amount of money. Some businesses may get a return on their investment of a few hundred dollars, but many huge businesses spend tens of thousands of dollars per month on pay-per-click advertising.

Though many business owners think that running Google ads cost too much, which makes them not so good choice for startups. However, the truth is that Google ads are very affordable and easily executable for startups. Google Ads marketing is ideal for finding highly targeted (based on search intent) clients for just a few dollars.

How Does A Digital Marketing Agency Help Startups With Google Ads?

A digital marketing agency for startups can help you carve out a well-thought Google Ads strategy for your business to give it necessary exposure in the quickest possible way. The best thing is that you will be charged only if someone clicks on your ad.

It is not like Google Ads would just work fine for your new businesses. There are lots of factors that need to be looked at before determining the feasibility of running Google ads. This is where a digital marketing agency can lend the much-needed help to startups by helping them find out if Google Ads investment is a profitable strategy for them.

Content Marketing

It refers to storytelling and kind of information-sharing tactics that tend to fuel brand awareness. This kind of digital marketing aims at generating interest among the readers and driving them towards an action like subscribing to a newsletter, buying a product, submitting the contact information, and others.

Here the term ‘Content’ refers to a number of text-based elements like blogs, articles, landing pages, white papers, videos, and much more. Content marketing is not just about creating and sharing the content, but the shared content should be persuasive enough to serve the purpose.

Content Marketing Strategy for Startups

It is important to note that content marketing works in conjunction with other types of digital marketing. For example, it makes the use of keywords finalized under an SEO strategy to make the content piece more effective and value-driven. By analyzing your content marketing strategy, you can answer several questions like – What kind of content your audience is interested in, which content piece drives more sales, what are the things that your readers love to search on your website, and more.

Content marketing, contrary to Google’s pay-per-click advertising, is typically a long-term strategy. Marketers accumulate a library of material (text, video, podcasts, and so on) over time that will continue to drive traffic to the site via search engines.

How Does a Digital Marketing Company Help Startups with Content Marketing

You can create more material for your website’s blog and social media channels with the aid of content marketing, a useful digital marketing technique for startups. Your SEO and SMO activities are supported by content marketing as well.

  • Boost brand awareness

  • Make your brand known.
  • Boost your brand’s credibility

Email Marketing Strategy for Startups

Affiliate Marketing Strategy for Startups

Affiliate marketing is also known as influencer marketing and intends to bring customers and businesses closer.

Affiliate marketing rests on industry experts’ and social media influencers’ ever-increasing popularity. Your company will partner with these third-party influencers in exchange for money to market your products or services. Influencers will engage their audiences through posts, blogs, or videos as part of a collaboration to bring in more business and generate new leads for your company.

How Does a Digital Marketing Agency Help with Affiliate marketing for Startups?

In simple words, a digital marketing agency follows a typical approach to leverage affiliate marketing. Though there are several steps and stakeholders in affiliate marketing, the most crucial one is to choose affiliates.

An experienced digital marketing agency can help startups find genuine affiliates who can provide access to different traffic sources within their networks. This, in turn, helps them to boost their sales promisingly.
